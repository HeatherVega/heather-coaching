What this is

The work that surrounds the medicine.

Psychedelic and plant medicine experiences have the potential to crack open what nothing else has been able to reach. They can illuminate what has been hidden, dissolve what has been rigid, and return you to a depth of yourself you may not have felt in years. But the experience itself is only the opening. What you do with it determines everything.

Integration is the careful, supported work of taking what arose in the journey and weaving it into how you actually live. The insights need a home in your body, your behavior, your relationships. Without that support, even the most profound experience can fade back into the familiar. With the right guidance, it becomes the turning point everything else organizes around.

I bring nearly two decades of personal ceremonial experience and deep knowledge of these medicines to this work. You are not explaining yourself to someone who has only read about this. You are working with someone who knows this terrain from the inside, and who will hold you with warmth, steadiness, and genuine understanding every step of the way.

How this unfolds

The arc of the work

Preparation

Two to six sessions in the weeks before your experience. We clarify intention, settle your nervous system, name what might come up, and build the steadiness that lets you meet whatever arises with presence rather than fear. You will not go in alone.

The threshold

A session shortly before and shortly after the experience itself. We do not go in cold and we do not come out into nothing. The edges of the experience deserve as much care as what is in the middle.

Integration

The longer arc, typically three to six months of regular sessions. Working with what arose, tending to what got stirred up, and helping the insights become embodied rather than just remembered. This is where the real transformation happens.

The slow weave

Real integration is not a single conversation. It is the patient, supported process of letting your relationships, choices, and sense of self slowly reorganize around what you have come to know. We walk that path together, at whatever pace you need.
