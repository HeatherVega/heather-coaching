What this is

Held every step of the way.

Psychedelic and plant medicine experiences have the potential to be profoundly transformative. They can illuminate what has been unconscious, dissolve what has been rigid, and open doorways to parts of yourself you may not have been able to reach any other way. They can also be disorienting, challenging, and deeply stirring. What makes the difference is not just the experience itself. It is the quality of support surrounding it.

I offer preparation, guidance, and integration support for those working with plant medicines and expanded state experiences. I bring nearly two decades of personal ceremonial experience to this work, alongside deep training in trauma-informed care, somatic healing, and psychedelic therapy. You are not navigating this with someone who has only read about it. You are working with someone who has lived it, who understands the terrain from the inside, and who will hold you with genuine warmth and steadiness throughout.

I also offer private intentional ceremony for life transitions, grief, and threshold moments that ask for something more than a regular conversation. Sacred space. Deliberate form. Full presence. Designed together to fit exactly what you are moving through.

What this includes

The shape of the support

Preparation

Getting ready for what you are about to enter. Clarifying intention, settling the nervous system, naming what might arise, and building the inner steadiness that lets you meet the experience with presence instead of fear.

Journey preparation and support

Experienced, grounded, compassionate guidance before and around expanded state experiences. Someone who knows this territory deeply and can help you prepare, set intention, and process what arises.

Integration

The careful, patient work of weaving what emerged into how you actually live. Helping the insights find a home in your body, your relationships, and your daily life so the experience becomes lasting transformation.

Threshold ceremony

For the passages that deserve more than a regular Tuesday. A grief that needs witnessing. An identity being released. A new chapter being entered. Intentional space designed together to honor exactly what you are moving through.

Grief and release work

Held space for loss, old or recent, visible or unnamed. With breath, witness, and the slow, safe weight of finally being met in what you have been carrying alone.
