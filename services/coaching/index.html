What this is

Shaped by your nervous system. Paced by what is true.

The patterns that keep repeating no matter how hard you try to change them. The exhaustion of a life built on top of wounds that were never truly healed. The quiet knowing that who you have become is not who you actually are.

That knowing is not self-criticism. It is the part of you that still remembers your wholeness. And it is right.

What you have been living is not your essence. It is what you built to survive. The patterns, the defenses, the identity formed around pain were never who you are. They were how you made it through. This work is the process of putting them down and returning to the self that was always underneath them.

This isn't talk therapy, and it isn't life coaching. It's slower, deeper work that reaches the places those approaches can't — your body, your nervous system, and the identity you built around pain long before you ever had words for it.

Transformational coaching at this level is not about thinking your way to a better life. It is about healing what has been underneath the thinking all along. We go to the root. We heal it there. And we do it together, at the pace your body can actually integrate, held by someone who has walked every part of this road herself.

What we work with

The terrain of this practice

Shadow work

Meeting the disowned, denied, and unconscious parts of yourself with steadiness rather than shame. Letting what has been in the dark begin to inform how you live rather than secretly run it.

Attachment wounding and trauma bonding

Tracing the patterns of how you bond, protect, withdraw, and reach. Gently reshaping the relational templates that were formed in pain and no longer serve who you are becoming.

Grief integration

Tending to losses old and new. The visible ones and the ones that never had a name. So they can finally move through you instead of staying lodged.

Inner-child healing

Returning to the younger places in you that are still carrying the weight. Offering the kind of presence and attention they did not receive the first time.

Addiction and recovery

Going beneath the substance or behavior to what it was protecting you from. Healing the root so recovery becomes transformation, not just abstinence.

Compassionate inquiry

Questions that get below the story you have been told about yourself and below the one you have been telling yourself. What is underneath those is where the real work begins.

How sessions unfold

The shape of the work

We begin with a discovery call

A complimentary 20-minute conversation to hear what is bringing you here and feel out whether this is the right fit. There is no pressure and no commitment. Just a real conversation between two people seeing if this work makes sense.

Sessions are 60 to 90 minutes

Held weekly or biweekly, online via video, with occasional in-person availability in Austin, TX. Sessions move between dialogue, somatic attention, and stillness, paced to what the day asks for. Single sessions are available, though most clients find the deeper work happens over time.

The work unfolds at your pace

Many clients choose to work in defined arcs, three months, six months, or longer, because the form gives the work room to deepen. We will find the shape that fits your life and your readiness together.

Pricing is shared in conversation

Because each engagement is shaped to the individual, pricing is discussed once we have had a chance to connect. A limited number of reduced-fee spots are available each quarter for those who need them.
