This agreement is entered into between Heather McCan, doing business as
Illuminated Integration ("Coach"), and the person signing below ("Client").

Please read this agreement carefully. By signing, you confirm that you
understand and agree to all terms below.

---

1. NATURE OF SERVICES

Heather McCan offers trauma-informed transformational coaching, psychedelic
integration support, addiction recovery coaching, and somatic healing services.

Coaching is a collaborative, forward-focused relationship designed to support
personal growth, healing, and transformation. It is not a substitute for
psychotherapy, psychiatric care, medical treatment, legal advice, or financial
advice.

If you are currently in crisis or require clinical mental health care, Coach
will support you in finding appropriate professional resources.

---

2. WHAT COACHING IS NOT

Coaching is distinct from therapy or counseling. Coach is not a licensed
therapist or psychologist. Sessions are not therapy sessions and do not
constitute a therapeutic relationship as defined by state licensing boards.

Nothing in our work together should be interpreted as a diagnosis, treatment
plan, or medical recommendation of any kind.

---

3. SESSIONS

Session length, frequency, and format will be agreed upon between Coach and
Client at the outset of the engagement.

Sessions are held via video call (online) or in person in Austin, TX,
depending on the service and mutual agreement.

---

4. FEES AND PAYMENT

Fees are discussed and agreed upon prior to the start of the engagement.
Payment is due before or at the time of each session unless a package
arrangement has been agreed upon in writing.

For package arrangements, payment is due in full (or per agreed installment
schedule) before sessions begin.

Fees are non-refundable once a session has taken place. Unused sessions in a
package are non-refundable after 30 days of the purchase date unless
exceptional circumstances are agreed upon in writing by both parties.

---

5. CANCELLATION, RESCHEDULING, AND NO-SHOW POLICY

Client agrees to provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or
rescheduling requests.

Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the full session
fee, except in cases of genuine emergency. Coach reserves the right to
determine what constitutes an emergency on a case-by-case basis and will
always aim to be reasonable and compassionate.

No-shows — defined as failing to appear for a scheduled session without any
prior notice — will be charged the full session fee. No exceptions.

After two no-shows, Coach reserves the right to require prepayment for all
future sessions or to terminate the coaching relationship entirely.

Coach reserves the right to reschedule sessions when necessary and will
provide as much notice as possible.

---

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

All information shared by Client in sessions is treated with strict
confidentiality. Coach will not disclose any information shared by Client to
any third party without Client's explicit written consent, with the following
exceptions required by law:

   a. If Coach has reason to believe Client is at imminent risk of harm to
      themselves or others.

   b. If disclosure is required by a court of law or other legal authority.

Coach may consult with a professional supervisor or mentor regarding session
content for the purpose of professional development. All such consultations
are conducted without identifying information.

---

7. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

   - Arrive on time and prepared for sessions.
   - Communicate openly and honestly to the best of their ability.
   - Take responsibility for their own choices, decisions, and actions.
   - Notify Coach of any changes in mental health, medications, or
     circumstances that may affect the work.
   - Reach out between sessions if urgent support is needed, with the
     understanding that Coach is not a crisis resource and response time
     may vary.

---

8. PSYCHEDELIC INTEGRATION SPECIFIC TERMS

Where services include psychedelic preparation or integration support,
Client confirms that:

   - Coach does not provide, source, or facilitate access to any controlled
     or scheduled substances.
   - Coach's role is education, preparation, and integration support only.
   - Client takes full personal responsibility for any choices made regarding
     the use of any substances.

---

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Coach makes no guarantees regarding specific outcomes from coaching or
integration work. Results vary by individual and depend on many factors
including Client's own effort, openness, and circumstances.

Coach's total liability to Client for any reason shall not exceed the total
fees paid by Client in the 30 days preceding the event giving rise to the
claim.

---

10. TERMINATION

Either party may end this agreement at any time with reasonable notice.
Client will be responsible for payment of any sessions already completed.
Unused prepaid sessions will be refunded on a pro-rated basis if Coach
terminates the agreement, except in cases of Client misconduct.

---

11. GOVERNING LAW

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Texas, United States.

---

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This document constitutes the entire agreement between Coach and Client
regarding the services described herein and supersedes any prior
conversations or representations.
