Before you write

There's no wrong way to begin. A sentence is enough. If it's easier, here are a few questions you can answer in your note:

  • What's prompting you to reach out now?
  • What are you hoping this work will change for you?
  • Is there a recent experience you'd like support integrating?
  • Anything you want me to know about how you'd like to be met?
