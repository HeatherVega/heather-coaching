ABOUT THIS WORK

What we do together is not therapy. It is not life coaching. It is something
deeper than both — a sustained, supported process of going to the root of
what has been quietly running your life and changing it there.

Whether you are healing trauma, navigating addiction recovery, integrating
a plant medicine experience, or simply knowing that something beneath the
surface has never been truly met — you are in the right place.

This work is real. It asks something of you. And it will give back more than
you put in.

---

WHAT TO EXPECT

Every session is different. Some sessions will feel like breakthroughs.
Others will feel slow, tender, or uncertain. Both are the work. There is no
wrong way to show up. Come as you are, every time.

Sessions typically move between:
- Dialogue and compassionate inquiry
- Somatic awareness and body-based practices
- Nervous system regulation
- Silence and stillness when that is what is needed

You will never be pushed further than your nervous system is ready to go.
Safety is not a luxury in this work. It is the foundation of it.

---

BEFORE OUR FIRST SESSION

Please take a few minutes to reflect on the following:

1. What is bringing you here right now?
   What has shifted, broken open, or become too much to keep carrying?

2. What do you most hope this work will change?
   Not what you think you should hope for — what do you actually want?

3. What do you most need from me as your guide?
   How do you want to be met? What helps you feel safe?

4. Is there anything important I should know before we begin?
   Medical history, current medications, recent experiences, anything that
   feels relevant.

You do not need to have answers. These are just invitations to arrive
with some intention already in motion.

PRACTICAL DETAILS

Sessions
Online via Google Meet or Zoom, with in-person availability in Austin, TX.
You will receive a link before each session. Please join from a private,
comfortable space where you will not be interrupted.

Timing
Please arrive on time. Sessions begin and end at the scheduled time out of
respect for both of us.

Cancellations and No-Shows
Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.
Late cancellations and no-shows are charged the full session fee.
Two no-shows will require prepayment for all future sessions.

Payment
Payment is due before or at the time of each session unless a package
arrangement has been agreed upon. You will receive a payment link via email.

Communication Between Sessions
I am available by email for brief questions or reflections between sessions.
I am not a crisis resource. If you are in immediate distress, please contact
the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Everything you share with me is held in strict confidence. I will not
disclose any information about our work together to anyone without your
explicit written consent, with the following legal exceptions:

- If I have reason to believe you are at imminent risk of harm to yourself
  or others.
- If disclosure is required by a court of law.
