About

Hello, I'm Heather.

I did not find this work. This work found me.

I am a trauma survivor. Childhood trauma. Trauma bonding. Attachment wounding. Abandonment. Love addiction. Substance addiction. Anxiety. Depression. Suicidal ideation. CPTSD. Dissociation. The shadow work that nobody wants to do but everything depends on. I have lived inside the patterns, the shame, the survival strategies, and the particular exhaustion of a life built around wounds that were never truly healed.

And then came the dark night of the soul.

It was in that darkness, through the deep work of my own healing journey, that I found the way through. It was in illuminating the darkness that I discovered what I now know to be true: the intersection of deep therapeutic work and plant medicine opened what nothing else could reach. It cracked open what had been buried and led me back to myself. What I found on the other side was not just healing. It was wholeness.

That journey is not separate from the work I do with clients. It is the work. It is what allows me to sit across from someone carrying what has never been spoken and know exactly where they are. Not from a textbook. From experience.

I built a professional practice on top of that foundation. Trauma-informed transformational coaching. Psychedelic integration. Addiction recovery support. Somatic healing. Nearly two decades of ceremonial practice. Over 15 years of therapeutic bodywork. Graduate-level training in psychedelic therapy. Every tool in service of one thing: helping you illuminate what has been hidden, heal it at the root, and return to the person you were always meant to be.

If any part of what I have named lives inside your story too, you are exactly who this work is for.

How I work

What shapes our time together

Root cause, not symptom management

We go to what is actually underneath. The patterns, the survival strategies, the hidden wounds that have been running the show. That is where lasting change lives.

Nervous system first

Real transformation cannot be forced or rushed. We work at the pace your body can actually integrate. Safety is not a luxury in this work. It is the condition for it.

Trauma-informed, always

Consent, choice, and your own authority over your experience are foundational. Nothing is imposed. You set the pace. I hold the space.

Direct and deeply compassionate

I will not soften the work into something more comfortable than it needs to be. And I will hold you with genuine care while we do it. Both are true at once.
