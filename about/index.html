About

Hello, I'm Heather.

Trauma-Informed Transformational Coach. Psychedelic Integration Specialist. Somatic Practitioner. Licensed Massage Therapist. And someone who has walked this path herself.

I help people get to the root. Not the surface patterns, not the coping strategies, but what is actually underneath — what has been suppressed, hidden, and avoided, sometimes for decades. That is where real transformation lives.

My work draws on over 15 years as a Licensed Massage Therapist specializing in somatic and trauma-informed bodywork, graduate-level training in psychedelic therapy, certification as a Transformational Addiction Recovery Coach, and nearly two decades of personal ceremonial practice within the Santo Daime tradition.

This path is also deeply personal. My own journey through trauma, loss, addiction, and profound transformation is not separate from the work I do. It is the foundation of it. I know what it takes to face what you have been avoiding. And I know what becomes possible when you do.

I work intuitively, relationally, somatically, and spiritually — always trauma-informed and always grounded. Clients often tell me they feel deeply seen, emotionally safe, and able to access parts of themselves they have been disconnected from for years.

How I work

Principles that shape our time together

Root cause, not symptom management

We go beneath the surface to illuminate what is actually driving your pain, patterns, and disconnection. Lasting change requires getting to what is real.

Nervous system first

We work at the pace your body actually allows. Real transformation cannot be forced. It unfolds when you feel safe enough to go deeper.

Trauma-informed, always

Consent, choice, and agency are foundational. Nothing is forced. You are the authority on your own experience.

Direct and compassionate

Real change asks us to face what is true. I will not soften the work into something more comfortable than it needs to be. And I will hold you with care while we do it.
